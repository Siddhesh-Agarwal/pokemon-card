Venusaur

Venusaur is a Grass/Poison-type Pokémon introduced in Generation I. It is known as the Seed Pokémon. Venusaur's evolutionary family is the only starter Pokémon evolutionary family to have the Poison-type. Mega Venusaur has the highest base stat total of all Poison-type Pokémon.
